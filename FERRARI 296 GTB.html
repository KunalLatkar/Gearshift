FERRARI 296 GTB

The Redefinition of Performance

Experience the pinnacle of hybrid hypercar technology with the Ferrari SF90 Stradale.

Specifications

  • Engine: 2.99 L V6 Twin-Turbocharged Petrol with Electric Motor
  • Horsepower: 1,000 hp (combined)
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Automatic
  • Acceleration: 0-100 km/h: 2.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: Over 200 mph
  • Electric Range: Up to 16 miles

The Ferrari 296 GTB is a revolutionary mid-engine PHEV (Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle) sports car. It marks Ferrari's first foray into a V6 architecture for a two-seater since the iconic Dino. The 296 GTB boasts a powerful combination of a 645bhp 2.9-liter twin-turbo V6 engine seamlessly integrated with a 164bhp electric motor, resulting in a staggering 1,000 horsepower. This innovative powertrain delivers exceptional performance while maintaining impressive efficiency with the ability to travel up to 16 miles on electric power alone. The 296 GTB stays true to Ferrari's heritage of driver engagement and dynamic handling. The car features advanced aerodynamic elements for optimal downforce, an E-Diff 3 electric differential for superior control, and a fully digital cockpit designed for intuitive interaction.
